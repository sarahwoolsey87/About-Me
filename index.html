Welcome to my page. My name is Sarah and I'm a Northern Irish girl, currently living in Liverpool but hoping to move to Spain in September. I've worked in education for the last 12 years in teaching, leadership and consultancy as an English specialist. Now I am in search of new adventures.

I like to continually strive towards self-improvement and learn new things. I'm currently studying Spanish to prepare for my travels. I've recently become interested in software development and I'm ambitious to create some educational applications in the near future.
